Features

Automatic Skips

Say goodbye to interruptions as AdBlitz automatically skips skippable ads on YouTube videos.

Ad Skimming

For non-skippable ads, AdBlitz skims through them at an accelerated speed (e.g., 16x), saving you valuable time.

Multi-Ad Skipping

Handles multiple ads with ease, keeping your viewing flow uninterrupted.

Background Magic

Works seamlessly in the background, providing a smooth and hassle-free viewing experience.

Lightweight

Easy to install and use, without any unnecessary bloat.
